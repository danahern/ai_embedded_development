# Nrf54l15 Learnings

- **ELF flashing can fail on nRF54L15 RRAM** — probe-rs sometimes fails to flash ELF files on nRF54L15 (RRAM-based flash). Fall back to `.hex` files: `flash_program(session_id, file_path="build/zephyr/zephyr.hex")`.
- **nRF54L15 RRAM flash quirks** — - `flash_program` with `.hex` works directly without prior erase
